WELCOME TO THE MATRIX

Greetings, cyber warriors and digital defenders! You've successfully connected to BSides South Jersey's mainframe. This is not your average cybersecurity conference - we're going old school, embracing the vintage aesthetic of the computing pioneers who laid the foundation for our digital world.

BSides South Jersey brings together hackers, security professionals, students, and curious minds for a day of knowledge sharing, networking, and exploration of the latest trends in information security. Whether you're a seasoned pentester or just beginning your journey into the world of cybersecurity, you'll find something valuable in our retro-terminal environment.
