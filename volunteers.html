Volunteers

> JOIN OUR TEAM

BSides South Jersey is powered by passionate volunteers who share a commitment to building a stronger cybersecurity community. Whether you're a security professional, student, or enthusiast, we welcome your help in creating an exceptional conference experience for attendees from across the region.

We accept both local and remote volunteers throughout the year. All volunteers must be at least 18 years of age.

> WHERE WE NEED YOUR HELP

Ticket Sales & Registration
Help manage ticket sales, attendee registration, and check-in processes. Support our efforts to make the registration experience smooth and welcoming for all participants.

Social Media & Marketing
Assist with content creation, community engagement, and spreading the word about BSides South Jersey across various social media platforms. Help us reach more people in the cybersecurity community.

After Party Planning
Help organize our post-conference networking event. Coordinate with venues, manage logistics, and ensure our attendees have a memorable evening of connection and celebration.

Capture The Flag (CTF) Competition
Support our CTF competition by helping with challenge development, infrastructure setup, participant support, and scoring. Perfect for those with technical security skills.

Villages & Tables
Assist in organizing and staffing our specialized villages and activity tables. Help create engaging hands-on experiences for attendees exploring different areas of cybersecurity.

Food & Venue Coordination
Support logistics including catering coordination, vendor management, and venue setup. Help ensure our attendees are well-fed and our space is organized and welcoming.

Merchandise & Swag
Help design, order, and distribute conference merchandise and swag. Manage inventory and assist with distribution at the event.

READY TO VOLUNTEER?

We're looking for dedicated volunteers to help us create an amazing conference experience. Fill out our volunteer application and tell us where you'd like to contribute. We'll match your skills and interests with our needs and reach out with next steps.

Requirements: Must be 18 years of age or older
Commitment: Flexible - varies by role and timeframe
Location: Remote and local opportunities available

APPLY TO VOLUNTEER

> VOLUNTEER BENEFITS

• Free conference admission
• Behind-the-scenes access to conference operations
• Networking opportunities with speakers and attendees
• Professional development and leadership experience
• The satisfaction of supporting your local cybersecurity community

> WHAT TO EXPECT

• Application Review: We'll review your application and reach out within 1-2 weeks
• Onboarding: Brief orientation and introduction to your team and responsibilities
• Flexible Commitment: Time commitment varies by role - some ongoing, some event-day only
• Team Communication: Regular updates via email and team channels
• Support: You'll have a team lead available to answer questions and provide guidance

> WHY VOLUNTEER?

BSides conferences exist because of volunteers like you. By joining our team, you're not just helping run an event - you're contributing to the growth of the cybersecurity community, creating opportunities for learning and connection, and helping to make our field more accessible and inclusive.

Whether you can contribute a few hours or want to be deeply involved throughout the year, we have opportunities that fit your schedule and interests. Every role is important, and every volunteer makes a difference.

> QUESTIONS?

If you have questions about volunteering for BSides South Jersey or want to learn more before applying, we'd love to hear from you. Reach out to our volunteer coordinator and we'll get back to you promptly.
