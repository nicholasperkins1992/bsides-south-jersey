> CALL FOR SPEAKERS

BSides South Jersey is seeking passionate cybersecurity professionals, researchers, and practitioners to share their knowledge and expertise with our community. Whether you're a seasoned speaker or presenting for the first time, we encourage you to submit your talk proposal.

> WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR

• Threat hunting, OSINT, incident response, and red/blue/purple team tactics
• Vulnerability research and exploit development
• Cloud security, DevSecOps, and secure coding practices
• Privacy, compliance, and security policy challenges
• Security culture, leadership, and community building
• Case studies, war stories, or lessons learned from the field
• AI/ML in cybersecurity and emerging tech trends

> PRESENTATION FORMAT

Standard Talks (25 minutes): Concise presentations with detailed technical content and Q&A

SUBMIT YOUR PROPOSAL

Ready to share your expertise? We're currently accepting speaker proposals for our upcoming conference. All submissions will be reviewed by our program committee.

Submission Deadline: January 1st, 2026
Notification Date: January 16th, 2026

> SPEAKER BENEFITS

• Complimentary conference admission
• Networking opportunities with cybersecurity professionals
• Exposure within the regional security community
• Professional development and speaking experience

> SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

• Provide a clear, descriptive title for your presentation
• Include a detailed abstract (300-500 words preferably)
• List any technical requirements or equipment needed
• Disclose any potential conflicts of interest
• All content must be original or properly attributed
