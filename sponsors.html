PARTNERSHIP PROTOCOL

BSides South Jersey operates on the principle of community-driven cybersecurity education. Our sponsors are more than financial supporters - they are partners in our mission to advance the cybersecurity field through knowledge sharing, skill development, and professional networking.

We offer multiple sponsorship tiers designed to provide maximum value for your investment while supporting our vibrant cybersecurity community. Each tier includes comprehensive benefits to showcase your organization's commitment to cybersecurity excellence.
